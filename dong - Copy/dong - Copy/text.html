WHAT IS BADONKADONKEYKONG?

Badonkadonkeykong ($DONG) is a token on the Binance Smart Chain that aims to reward holders in BUSD rather than forcing them to sell off their stack in order to profit. Launched by a dev that had seen his phat memecoin stacks get reduced to dust over and over again while being unable to bring himself to take profits and potentially damage the communities he’d become attached to, DONG allows profits to be taken passively and responsibly in a way that benefits all holders instead of just a few lucky, early whales. A 1% max wallet insulates holders from the threat of large dumps and the liquidity lock of 100 years allows holders to feel safe from rug pulls while a 3% tax on every transaction to the LP ensures a ever rising and stabilizing price floor.
